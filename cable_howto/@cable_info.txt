my cable recommendations from http://www.reichelt.de

RGBI cable:
Art.Nr. AK 2187
D-SUB Kabel, 1:1, 9-pol., Stecker/Stecker, 3m
cut the cable into half and use one for the RGBI cable

Pin	color	info
++++++++++++++++++++
1	black	GND
	+screen
2	brown	GND
3	red	Red
4	orange	Green
5	yellow	Blue
6	green	Intensity
7	blue	Monochrome
8	purple	HSync
9	white	VSync

strip the cable isolation that the wires 1-9 are 5 cm long.
I connected the cable screen to GND (black) as shown.
the pictures shows the whole process.  

DIN-8 cable:
Art.Nr. MAS 80S 
DIN-Stecker, 8-polig, Hufeisenform
use the rest of the D-SUB cable to build the A/V cable 

Pin	color	info
++++++++++++++++++++
1	black	Luminance (luma)
2	brown	GND
	+white+screen
3	red	Audio OUT
4	orange	Video OUT
5	yellow	Audio IN
6	green	Chromiance (chroma)
7	blue	not connected
8	purple	+5 Volt

strip the cable isolation that the wires 1-9 are 3,5 cm long.
I connected the cable screen to GND (brown+white) as shown.
the pictures shows the whole process.  
a link to the pinout of the A/V socket:
https://www.c64-wiki.com/wiki/File:AudioVideo_8pol_Pinbelegung.gif

SCART cable:
Art.Nr. AK 315-15
Scart Kabel 21-pol Scart Stecker, 1,5 m
or 
Art.Nr. AK 315-30
Scart Kabel 21-pol Scart Stecker, 3 m

cut the lenght you wanna use and look at the pictures.
they show you where you can find the right wires. 
you just need 12 wires of the 21 wires.
a link to the pinout of a SCART Plug:
http://www.nairam.sk/scart.jpg

it is not easy to solder but possible :)
